What is DigiPen?

DigiPen Applied Computer Graphics School is a post-secondary education facility that focuses on the technological and engineering process of creating interactive multimedia programs, providing top quality education in the field of video game programming and computer graphics. It is the goal of DigiPen to provide the best environment to its students so that they will enjoy school and graduate ready to enter a fun and exciting profession.

DigiPen Applied Computer Graphics School is located in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada and is registered with the B.C. Private Post-Secondary Education Commission.

The DigiPen Institute of Technology - Opening Fall 1997

On September 6, 1996, DigiPen and Nintendo announced that DigiPen will be opening the DigiPen Institute of Technology that will be offering the world's first Baccalaureate Degree of Science in Real Time Interactive Simulation. For more information, check out the press release.
